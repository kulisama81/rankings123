# Distinctive logo & wordmark identity system

Replace the current text-only "RANKINGS 123" with a distinctive, memorable logo/wordmark that works across all contexts: nav (desktop/mobile), favicon, OG images, and social. - Maintains legibility in dark + light + all 3 design variants

- No third-party logo generator artifacts (authentic, custom design)
