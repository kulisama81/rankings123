# Homepage engagement improvement (reduce 75% bounce rate)

Homepage has 75% bounce rate vs 20% on World Cup page and 11% on ATP Live (per analytics). Users landing on homepage aren't finding what they need. Improvements: (1) Hero section showing LIVE status indicators (tournaments happening NOW with live match count), (2) Quick links to top player pages when built, (3) Recent ranking changes callout (biggest movers today), (4) Upcoming major events section, (5) Better mobile CTAs. Goal: reduce bounce rate from 75% to <40% and increase avg session duration from 11s to 30s+.

## Acceptance Criteria

✓ Homepage redesign shipped
✓ Live status indicators showing active tournaments
✓ Recent ranking changes / biggest movers callout
✓ Upcoming events section (next Grand Slam, World Cup knockout, etc.)
✓ Clear CTAs for each sport (View ATP Live, View World Cup, etc.)
✓ Mobile-optimized layout
✓ A/B test or analytics tracking on bounce rate + session duration
✓ Target: bounce rate <40%, session duration >30s

## Notes

**2026-06-24T13:50:27Z**

ROI Justification (First Principles):

FUNDAMENTAL TRUTH: Homepage is the front door. 75% bounce rate = 3 out of 4 visitors leave immediately = massive waste of traffic.

ANALYTICS DATA (last 28 days):
- Homepage: 55 views, 11.0s avg session, 75% bounce (BAD)
- World Cup: 29 views, 358.8s avg session, 20% bounce (EXCELLENT)
- ATP Live: 12 views, 15.6s avg session, 11% bounce (EXCELLENT)

ROOT CAUSE: Homepage doesn't answer 'what's happening NOW?' Users don't know where to go or what to click.

DIRECT SOLUTION: Make homepage LIVE and ACTION-ORIENTED - show what's happening NOW (tournaments in progress, live matches, ranking changes today), with clear CTAs to each section.

IMPACT vs EFFORT:
- Impact: HIGH - 55 views/month × 75% saved = 40+ extra engaged sessions/month (growing as traffic scales)
- Effort: MEDIUM - redesign homepage with live indicators, dynamic content
- ROI: HIGH - every % of bounce rate saved = more sessions, more ad impressions, more page views

FIRST PRINCIPLES: Why do users come to rankings123? To know WHO'S #1 NOW and WHAT'S HAPPENING LIVE. Homepage should scream that, not be static navigation.
