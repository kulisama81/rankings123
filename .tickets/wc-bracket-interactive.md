# World Cup interactive knockout bracket

Visual knockout bracket tree with results, live status, upcoming matches, click-through to match pages

## Acceptance Criteria

Visual bracket tree (R16 → QF → SF → Final), shows results where available, 'live now' indicators, click match to navigate to match page; responsive on mobile (stackable or horizontal scroll); no placeholder matches (only show confirmed fixtures)

## Notes

**2026-06-26T13:48:53Z**

ROI: PEAK ENGAGEMENT during knockout rounds (starting soon). Visual brackets are the #1 way fans follow tournaments; drives repeat visits. First principles: users need to see 'who plays who next' and 'path to final' at a glance — text tables don't deliver this.
